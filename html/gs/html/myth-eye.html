The Black Eyed Children

The Black Eyed Children is an urban legend about unknown creatures that show in the form of children from the ages of 6 to 16. They look different but they all have pale skin and pure black eyes with no irises

The first sighting of these "children" was by Brian Bethel in 1996, and since then the tales of these creatures has only spread. These children have been seen hitchhiking asking for car rides or on front porches asking to come inside. Often to use a phone to call their mother or a ride home.

They will ask to come inside your house or car, as people have reported the children saying they needed to be invited to come inside. People always feel extreme fear and terror when they see the children’s eyes, and pets are always terrified of them. It is unknown what happens if you let in any of these children, but people that have seen these children feel as if they are the predators and we are the prey.
