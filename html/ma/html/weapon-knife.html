Knife

A knife is a small weapon with a cutting edge. Some are vary small and easily concealable making it a frequent weapson in assaults and attacks. It can be used very deadly, quickly, and quietly.

A great number of martial arts teach knife defense because of how deadly, how easy they are to hide, and how frequently they are used as weapons on the street.
