Below is a list of martial arts.

Keep in mind every style has its strengths and weaknesses. And not every school/ gym is going to teach the right way. Some schools teach in a non-practical way, and some teach incorrectly. That does not mean that that particular style is ineffective.

Everyone is different in their body and what works for them. Find a style or combination of style that suite you. Possibly try taking several different styles, remember everything that you learn and adapt what works for you into your skill set.
